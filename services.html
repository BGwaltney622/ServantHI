Servant Home Inspection Services

Servant Home Inspection is your reliable provider of home inspection services in Arlington, and the DFW Metroplex. Our certified expert will carry out a thorough inspection of the condition of the various components in your home. We are here to prevent your American dream of becoming a homeowner from turning into a terrifying experience. Our comprehensive inspection abides by the standards of practice for home inspectors established through the Texas Real Estate Commission (TREC). Some of our home inspection services include:

  • Fast Callback and Response

    We have personnel on the ground to respond to your concerns and inquiries, on the same day.

  • Inspection Report

    Upon completion of the inspection, we will send a copy of the inspection report with pictures to your email. Our inspection reports are easy to explain and analyze.

Structural Systems

The structural system of a home is one of the major essential components of the home. Let our qualified experts at Servant Home Inspection carry out a detail inspection of the structural system in your home. We will inspect the foundation, drainage, roof covering, attic, walls, floors, doors, windows, and fireplaces. Our home inspection services may be all you need to save yourself on expensive structural repairs and renovations.

Electrical Systems

Call on our expert at Servant Home Inspection to help check out the condition of the electrical system in your home. We will inspect your service entrance and panels (breaker box), branch circuits, connected devices, and identify deficiencies before you go on with the home purchase.

Heating and Air Conditioning Systems

The heating and cooling systems of a home is an essential aspect of the home, especially during extreme weather conditions. Save yourself from the expensive cost of repairing the HVAC system by hiring our experts at Servant Home Inspection to inspect the heating and air conditioning systems. We will inspect the vents and duct system and determine whether they are in good condition or not.

Plumbing System

Living in a home with a broken or damaged plumbing system can be very devastating. Leaking pipes, broken fixtures, damaged taps, blocked drains, can make life unbearable in the new home. Call on our expert at Servant Home Inspection to help inspect your plumbing supply, fixtures, drains, water heating equipment, kitchen sink, and hydro-massage therapy equipment.

Appliances

Furthermore, we can also help inspect the appliance in the home including dishwashers, ranges, cooktops, ovens, and garage door operators. We save you from any disappointment before moving into the home.

Optional Systems

At Servant Home Inspection, our inspection services will also help determine the state of your landscape irrigation (sprinkler) system, swimming pools, hot tubs, and outbuildings. We offer you the opportunity to investigate the home you have the intention of buying carefully.

Client Walkthrough

Also, we take you through the various parts of the property. During this process, you can examine the state of the equipment and facilities. You can also ask questions during the process. We will be eager to provide suitable answers to your questions.
